About MCA

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Surana College is a 2-year postgraduate program designed to develop advanced skills in computer science, software engineering, and emerging technologies. With a curriculum focused on real-world applications, industry projects, and modern labs, MCA graduates are prepared for top IT roles in software development, data science, and more.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: Any UG with Math
  • Focus: Software, Data Science, AI
  • Placement: 100% Assistance
  • Labs: Modern & Industry Aligned
